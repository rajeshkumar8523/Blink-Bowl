Privacy Policy

At Blink Bowl, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Personal information (name, phone, email)
  • Service details (booking info, location)
  • Payment data (secured by third-party providers)
  • Device and usage information

2. How We Use Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide our services (car wash, driver support, etc.)
  • Improve your experience and our offerings
  • Send updates, offers, and booking confirmations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Information may be shared only with:

  • Trusted third-party service providers
  • Law enforcement, if required by law

4. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance website functionality. You can disable them in your browser settings.

5. Security

We implement security measures to protect your data, though no method is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request access or deletion of your data
  • Update your contact preferences
  • Withdraw consent from marketing communications

7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 years. If discovered, we will delete it immediately.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted with the updated effective date.
