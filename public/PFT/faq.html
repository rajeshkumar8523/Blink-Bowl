Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Find answers to common questions about our services at Blink Bowl.

1. What is Blink Bowl?

Blink Bowl is a convenient platform offering car wash services, temporary drivers, and self-drive car rentals directly at your doorstep.

2. How do I book a service?

You can book through our website blinkbowl.in or contact us directly via call or WhatsApp. Booking confirmation will be sent via SMS or email.

3. What locations do you currently serve?

We operate in selected cities across India. You can check service availability on our homepage or while booking.

4. Do I need to be present during the car wash?

No. As long as your vehicle is accessible, our professionals will handle everything. We also send before/after photos on request.

5. How are your drivers verified?

All drivers undergo strict background checks, license verification, and driving skill assessments before joining our team.

6. What safety measures do you follow?

We ensure hygienic practices, masked professionals, and contactless services. For self-drive rentals, vehicles are sanitized before every trip.

7. What payment options are available?

We accept UPI, cards, net banking, and cash on delivery (COD) where available. Payment links are sent after service confirmation.

8. Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule up to 2 hours before your booking time. Contact our support team for quick changes.
