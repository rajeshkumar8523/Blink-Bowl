Car Washing Services

At Blink Bowl, we bring top-quality car cleaning right to your doorstep. Our Car Washing Services are designed to give your vehicle a spotless, showroom-ready finish using eco-friendly products, professional tools, and a trained team committed to excellence.

Types of Vehicles We Wash

At Blink Bowl, we provide comprehensive cleaning services for a wide range of vehicles. Whether it's your personal car or a commercial vehicle, our expert team is equipped to handle it all.

Our services cover standard vehicles like cars, bikes, and autos, as well as agricultural and heavy-duty machines such as tractors, harvesters, and JCBs. We also cater to larger vehicles including buses, lorries, mini buses, goods carriers, and DCM trucks.

No matter the type or size, every vehicle receives the same level of care and professional detailing to ensure a spotless, showroom-quality finish.

Convenient Doorstep Service

Forget the hassle of visiting a service center. Our mobile units bring complete washing and detailing directly to your location, saving you time while delivering quality service at your convenience.

Eco-Friendly Cleaning Solutions

We care about your vehicle and the environment. That’s why we use biodegradable, water-saving products and gentle cleaning techniques that protect your vehicle's paint while conserving resources.

Professional Team & Tools

Our care experts are trained in handling all types of vehicles using advanced tools and equipment. From high-pressure washes to vacuuming and waxing, we ensure precision and attention to detail.

Interior & Exterior Detailing

We go beyond just washing. Our detailing packages include dashboard cleaning, seat vacuuming, tire polishing, and exterior waxing – giving your vehicle a complete rejuvenation inside and out.

Affordable & Reliable
